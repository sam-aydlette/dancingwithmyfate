About The Book:

The book Dancing With My Fate is the culmination of many years of investigation of the world, sometimes slow and deliberate, more often manic and compulsive. The questions that I have asked, the classes I have taken from the school of hard-knocks, and finally, the insights I have discerned. A survival guide, yes, but more than that - the documentation of my philosophical, spiritual, practical metamorphoses into a creature that is uniquely at home in the toxic and seemingly nonsensical environment we find ourselves in.


About The Author:

Sam Aydlette is a former infantryman turned cybersecurity practitioner who has always been an amateur philosopher. He lives in the Washington DC metro area with his amazing family who make him very proud.
